Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to MechTech ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile application ("MechTech").

2. Information We Collect

To provide high-quality premium mechanic booking services, we collect limited personal details, including:

  • Profile Details: Your name, email address, and Google profile picture (retrieved via secure Google Sign-in authorization).
  • Location & Address: The service location details you manually enter (Flat No, Street, City, and Pincode) to dispatch mechanic assistance to your vehicle.
  • Vehicle Information: The type (Car, Bike, EV) and model of your vehicle selected for browsing services.
  • Booking History: Logs of your scheduled, in-progress, or completed services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following specific purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity securely and maintain your customer dashboard account.
  • To assign and direct physical mechanic services to your specified address.
  • To generate transaction records and manage booking timelines.
  • To contact you for support and service confirmations.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your details are stored securely using Google Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore database systems. We do not sell or trade your personal information to third parties. All sensitive network communication is encrypted over HTTPS.

5. User Rights & Controls

We believe in user control over personal data. You have the following rights:

  • You can update your name and phone number directly from the Edit Profile screen.
  • You can change your service address at any time from the Add Address screen.
  • You can request complete deletion of your account and related booking details by reaching out to our support team.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.
